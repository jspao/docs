## 响应式

```html
<div class="24 m:24 l:4 xl:4">screen</div>
```
- 响应式容器自适应
  xs|sm|md|lg|xl|2xl|3xl|4xl|5xl|6xl|7xl|8xl|9xl
- 响应式展示与隐藏
  block xs-block sm-block md-block lg-block xl-hidden 2xl-hidden
